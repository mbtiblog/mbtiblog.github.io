INTJs , ENTJs , INFP , ENFJ , ESFJ

INTJs (Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging) are a personality type that are often referred to as "architects" or "strategists." They are known for their intelligence, independence, and ability to think critically. They have a strong sense of logic and are often able to see patterns and connections that others may not. They are also known for their ability to plan for the future and to have a clear vision for what they want to accomplish. They can be very private and reserved, and are often more comfortable working independently rather than in a team setting.

ENTJs (Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking, Judging) are a personality type that are often referred to as "commanders" or "executives." They are known for their confidence, assertiveness, and ability to lead. They are natural leaders who are able to see the big picture and inspire others to work towards a common goal. They are also known for their strategic thinking and ability to make decisions quickly and effectively. They can be seen as ambitious, outgoing and commanding people.

INFPs (Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Perceiving) are a personality type that are often referred to as "mediators" or "healers." They are known for their empathetic and compassionate nature, as well as their strong sense of values and ideals. They are often able to understand and connect with others on a deep level, and are able to see the good in everyone. They are also known for their strong sense of creativity and ability to see the potential in things. They can be seen as quite sensitive and private people, who are often more comfortable working behind the scenes rather than in a leadership role.

ENFJs (Extraverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Judging) are a personality type that are often referred to as "teachers" or "mentors." They are known for their ability to inspire and motivate others, as well as their strong sense of empathy and compassion. They are often able to understand and connect with others on a deep level, and are able to see the good in everyone. They are also known for their strong sense of responsibility and desire to help others. They can be seen as outgoing and charismatic people, who are often more comfortable working in a leadership role rather than behind the scenes.

ESFJs (Extraverted, Sensing, Feeling, Judging) are a personality type that are often referred to as "providers" or "caregivers." They are known for their practicality, and their ability to take care of others. They are often able to understand and connect with others on a deep level, and are able to see the good in everyone. They are also known for their strong sense of responsibility and desire to help others. They can be seen as outgoing and caring people, who are often more comfortable working in a leadership role rather than behind the scenes.
